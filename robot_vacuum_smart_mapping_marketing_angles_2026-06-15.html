Research Notes grounded in live research - no invented data

Top Pain Points

  • Pet hair tangling around brushes every 2-4 weeks (requiring manual scissors intervention)
  • Cheap robots get stuck under furniture; owner comes home to an untouched room and a dead battery
  • Smart mapping gets corrupted after furniture moves or battery drain; requires full remap
  • Self-empty dock announces "complete" but dustbin is still full (dock suction failures)
  • Thresholds and rug edges cause immobilization ("Mount Everest to small wheels")
  • WiFi connectivity failures - won't connect to 5GHz routers; drops connection during runs
  • Cheap robots miss entire zones while over-cleaning small areas ("cleaned the same spot 12 times")
  • In pet-heavy homes, "30-day self-empty" fills in 10-14 days instead
  • Dust cloud from emptying the bin without a sealed dock (inhaled by the owner)
  • Social embarrassment: guest arrival with visible pet hair = panic scramble clean

Top Benefits (Verified)

  • HEPA self-emptying - 99.97% allergen containment, sealed bag, no dust cloud
  • Smart room-by-room mapping - remembers layout, cleans every zone systematically
  • Multi-floor memory - up to 4 floor plans remembered
  • Auto-lift mop pads on carpet transitions (no wet carpet edges)
  • Tangle-free brush design built for pet-hair households
  • App + voice control (Alexa/Google Home) - integrates into existing smart home routine
  • Time recovery: 50+ hours/year reclaimed from vacuuming
  • True hands-free: touch it once a month (dock bag change)
