Research Notes

Top Pain Points (Grounded in Research)

  • $90-300 hydrogen water machines break within 10-14 days, leaving buyers without their daily H2 and deeply skeptical of the category
  • Metallic/chalky aftertaste from tablets and generators -- taste is a recurring barrier to consistent use
  • Caffeine dependency with 2pm energy crashes -- seeking clean, sustained energy alternative
  • Post-workout soreness lasting 48+ hours, costing multiple training days per week
  • Hydrogen gas dissipates quickly -- hard to maintain consistently high PPM without fresh preparation
  • No industry standard for concentration -- buyers can't verify what they're actually consuming
  • "Snake oil" skepticism driven by small studies, dietitian skepticism, and category noise
  • Brain fog and afternoon cognitive fatigue that coffee addresses inadequately
  • Silent joint stiffness/aching that accumulates over years but has no acute cause to address
  • Price anxiety -- $90+/month feels unjustifiable without measurable personal confirmation of effect

  • Improved focus and cognitive clarity during work hours -- "more focused during work hours"
  • Reduced joint pain during exercise -- "joints don't ache as much after evening walks"
  • Ease of use vs. machines -- drop tablet, wait 3 minutes, drink -- no setup, no maintenance, travel-ready

  • 12 PPM H2 per tablet (backed by 25+ clinical studies per listing)

Key Objections to Pre-empt

  • PRIMARY: "Is this snake oil? Will I personally feel a difference?" -- address by leading with converted-skeptic voice, not clinical authority
  • Placebo uncertainty -- "benefits may be placebo"; address with 30-day challenge framing and specific, verifiable outcomes
  • No industry standard / no FDA regulation -- transparency on PPM and third-party testing is the counter
  • Failed for healthy people in studies -- focus messaging on specific populations and specific outcomes, not "general wellness"
  • Price anxiety -- ROI vs. machines ($1.16/day vs. $300 machine that breaks) is the counter-frame
  • Taste issues -- address proactively if product has clean taste; if not, avoid this angle
  • Hydrogen dissipation -- acknowledge and address: tablets are consumed within minutes, maintaining high PPM
