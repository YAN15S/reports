Customer Avatar

Situation: Has tried chiropractor ($2,500-4,000/yr), NSAIDs, heating pads, physical therapy - all gave only temporary relief. Sleep is disrupted 3-5x/week. Has given up hobbies (walking, gardening, cleaning). Feels hopeless and dependent on others.
Trigger: Sciatica flare woke them up at 3am, or nerve pain made it impossible to sit through a full work meeting. Looking for something that works differently from everything they have tried.

After State: Sleeping through the night, walking without stopping, sitting without the electric zap, doing activities they had given up
