Top Pain Points (from Research)
  • Ruining expensive cuts by guessing temperatures (emotional + financial pain)

  • Floating bags causing uneven cooking (beginner frustration)
  • Bag leaks diluting flavor and ruining texture
  • Time investment amplifies disappointment when results are bad
  • Searing failure after perfect sous vide cook (wet meat, wrong pan temp)
  • Residual cooking confusion - food continues cooking after removal
  • "Is it worth it?" paralysis before buying
  • BPA / plastic safety concerns during high-temp cooking
  • Dry, flavorless batch-cooked proteins for meal prep

  • Restaurant-quality results at home (what 3-Michelin-star kitchens use)

  • WiFi remote monitoring via Anova app (dual-band Gen 3)
  • App-guided recipes (built-in chef expertise, free tier)
  • Works with any existing pot - no new vessel needed
  • Dishwasher-safe removable stainless steel skirt
