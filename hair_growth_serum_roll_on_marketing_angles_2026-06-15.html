5 Research Notes

Top 10 Pain Points (grounded in research)

  • Hair falling out in the shower daily — drain cleaning becomes a ritual of dread ("the most emotionally honest test of hair loss")
  • Seeing scalp in current photos vs. 2 years ago — photo comparison is the undeniable gut-punch moment
  • Spent hundreds on biotin, vitamins, shampoos with no result — "tried everything" exhaustion and self-blame
  • "I don't feel feminine anymore" / "I don't recognize myself" — identity loss, not just cosmetic frustration
  • Serums are greasy or leave residue — dropper format fails compliance; women quit rather than deal with greasy hair
  • Applied it for 3 weeks, saw nothing, gave up — quit right before the growth phase starts (week 4-6 baby hairs)
  • Postpartum clumps in the shower — "I know it's normal but nothing prepared me for the clumps, I was already exhausted"
  • Hairdresser noticed and said something — third-party validation of what the woman had been denying
  • Baby hairs appear but don't thicken into real hair, so women stop using it at the wrong moment
  • Terrified it won't come back — fear of permanent follicle dormancy is the deepest emotional driver

Top Benefits (from product + competitor research)

  • Roll-on ball delivers formula directly to follicle — not down the hair shaft; rolling motion opens absorption pathways via micro-stimulation
  • Ingredient stack: Redensyl (proven efficacy), Aminexil (anti-fibrosis around follicle), Rosemary (blood circulation), Procapil + Anagain + Saw Palmetto (multi-pathway)
  • Mess-free, non-greasy, fast-absorbing — removes the daily application friction that kills serum compliance
  • Dual use: scalp (primary) + brows/edges/hairline (precision applicator enables targeting)
  • Consumable / resuppliable — 20-50ml rolls on finish in 4-6 weeks, natural reorder cadence with results-driven retention
  • K-beauty / Korean scalp-first philosophy — global trend credibility without requiring brand origination from Korea
  • Clean formula — paraben-free, sulfate-free, cruelty-free; appeals to health-conscious beauty buyer
  • Visible results: reduced shedding week 1-2, baby hairs week 4-6, visible density week 8-12
