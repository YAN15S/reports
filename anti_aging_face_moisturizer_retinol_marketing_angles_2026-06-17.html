Research Notes

Top Pain Points

  • Retinol irritation / adjustment period -- 2-6 weeks of peeling, redness, burning; women abandon before seeing results
  • Purging confusion -- breakouts after starting retinol; unsure if normal or product rejection
  • Wasted money on expensive serums with no visible improvement within 4-6 weeks
  • Topical collagen skepticism -- informed buyers know collagen molecules cannot penetrate skin; view it as marketing
  • Photosensitivity anxiety -- fear of accelerated sun damage from retinol use in warmer months
  • Heavy / greasy textures that don't layer well under makeup
  • Results plateau after 2-3 months -- skin adapts, improvement stalls
  • The daily mirror moment -- seeing visible aging even when feeling young and sharp inside

Top Benefits Sought

  • Visible wrinkle reduction -- fine lines and deep wrinkles noticeably smoother
  • Even, brighter skin tone -- reduction of age spots, sun damage, dullness
  • Firmer, more lifted appearance -- improved elasticity around jawline and cheeks
  • Hydration without heaviness -- plump skin that absorbs quickly and layers under makeup
  • Confidence in photos without filters or heavy concealer
