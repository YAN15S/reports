Does not describe herself as a "cleaning person" but wishes her home looked as clean as those videos.

Research Notes

Top Pain Points (from real reviews, Reddit, Slickdeals)

  • Grout that will not come clean no matter what -- the single most documented frustration
  • Cheap steam cleaners that produce warm mist, not real steam (nozzle temp below 200F)
  • Mold that keeps coming back because bleach only discolors the surface
  • Harsh chemicals with kids and pets present -- health anxiety is a real purchase driver
  • Physical toll of scrubbing -- knees, back, raw hands -- with no result
  • Review anxiety: the steam cleaner category has documented AI-generated reviews and same-day clusters
  • Short run time requiring constant cool-down and refill cycles
  • Leaking or defective units out of the box (known Dupray recall: 750K units, burn hazard)
  • Embarrassment about a home that looks dirty even after cleaning
  • The seven cleaning products under the sink that never work

Top Benefits (from reviews and content)

  • Grout restored to like-new in seconds -- the visual transformation is the #1 purchase driver
  • No chemicals required -- water only, kills bacteria at 230F+
  • Kills mold at the root, not just bleaches the surface color
  • 16 surfaces covered with one 16-piece kit
  • 15-second heat-up removes the psychological barrier to cleaning
  • Replaces 5-10 cleaning products (declutter identity benefit)
  • Kills dust mite allergens -- documented at 135F+
  • Safe for kids, pets, and allergy/asthma households
  • "I wish I had this sooner" is the most common post-purchase sentiment
