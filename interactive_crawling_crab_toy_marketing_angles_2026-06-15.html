What Parents Try That Fails

  • Forcing longer sessions (backfires, creates negative association)

  • Baby alone on floor with no engagement

  • Fancy tummy time mats (static, novelty wears off fast)
  • Lying next to baby face-to-face (requires mom's full attention)
  • Generic baby toys that light up when touched (reactive not proactive)
